Not a substitute for medical care

The data displayed through your Nightscout site is provided "as is" for your personal informational use. It is not a substitute for:

  • Professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment by a qualified healthcare provider;
  • The standalone clinical interpretation of CGM data by your endocrinologist or diabetes care team;
  • The use of medically certified devices for treatment decisions, where such devices are recommended by your clinician;
  • Emergency medical services in the event of a medical emergency.

Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition, and never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of something you observed via Seravi or Nightscout.

Accuracy of data

The accuracy of glucose readings displayed via your Nightscout site depends on:

  • The accuracy of your CGM sensor and its calibration;
  • The reliability of the uploader application you use (e.g. xDrip+, Loop, Spike, the Dexcom bridge, etc.);
  • Network connectivity between your devices and our servers;
  • The Nightscout software's own correctness.

Seravi does not validate, verify, or guarantee the accuracy of any data displayed. Data may be delayed, missing, duplicated, or incorrect. Treatment decisions must be based on validated, real-time measurements from medically certified devices, not on data displayed via Seravi.

This includes (but is not limited to) decisions about insulin dosing, food intake, exercise, sensor changes, and when to seek medical attention. Seravi accepts no liability for treatment decisions made using data hosted on our infrastructure.

Use by parents and guardians

When a parent or guardian uses Seravi to monitor diabetes data on behalf of a minor (a common use case in pediatric Type 1 diabetes), the same disclaimers apply: the parent or guardian is responsible for any treatment decisions, and the service does not replace clinical oversight by the child's healthcare team.
