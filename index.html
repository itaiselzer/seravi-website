Our promise

Built for people, not engineers

Nightscout is life-changing software — but setting it up has always asked too much of the people who need it most. Servers, secrets, broken free tiers, endless forum threads.

Seravi exists to make that disappear. We handle the hard parts so you can focus on what matters: seeing the numbers, sharing them with the people you trust, and getting on with your day.

European-hosted, privacy-first, and designed to be the easiest way to run Nightscout — for parents, partners, and anyone living with diabetes.

“The last thing you need is another technical project.”

That belief shapes every decision we make. If a step can be removed, we remove it. If something can be automatic, it is. Seravi is what Nightscout hosting should have been all along — calm, reliable, and out of your way.
